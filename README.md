# Ones and Zeros - Umbrel App Store

A community Umbrel app store containing the [Bitcoin Tax Calculator](https://github.com/onesandzeros-nz/bitcoin-tax) — a privacy-focused tool for calculating Bitcoin capital gains using the Weighted Average Cost (WAC) method for New Zealand IRD reporting.

## Installation

### 1. Open the Umbrel App Store

From your Umbrel dashboard, click the **App Store** icon.

### 2. Add the Community App Store

1. In the App Store, click the **three dots** (⋯) in the top-right corner
2. Select **Community App Stores**
3. Click **Add**
4. Paste the following URL:

```
https://github.com/onesandzeros-nz/onesandzeros-umbrel
```

5. Click **Add**

### 3. Install the App

1. You should now see **Ones and Zeros Apps** listed under community app stores
2. Find **Bitcoin Tax Calculator** and click **Install**
3. Wait for the installation to complete

### 4. Open the App

Once installed, open Bitcoin Tax Calculator from your Umbrel dashboard. Log in using the password shown on the app's info page in Umbrel (a deterministic password is generated automatically).

## What It Does

- Import CSV transaction data from Easy Crypto, Lightning Pay, Xapo, Kraken, and CoinCorner
- Calculate Weighted Average Cost (WAC) for NZ IRD tax reporting
- Manage NZ tax years (April 1 – March 31)
- Keep your full transaction history private — only share the final tax figures with your accountant

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your Umbrel in a SQLite database. Nothing leaves your device.
